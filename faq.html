F.A.Q

  • Who are you people?

    We're a group of young adults sharing what we know and giving back to the community. We're students, scientists, musicians, artists, soldiers, programmers, playwrights, and more.

  • Why are you doing this?

  • Will I need to pay to view lectures?

    No, and never. Just help us spread the word by telling a friend, or two! Find out more.

  • How do you know you're correct?

  • Do you have notes or sample questions?

    These things are in the pipeline. We don't have a delivery date just yet, but on the honour of the Cookie Monster, it will happen!

  • I've found an error! What can I do?

    Damn. That shouldn't have happened. We're very sorry! You can let us know about it here

  • I want to contribute. Take me! Take me!

    To work with us you must be crazy and crazy good. Not frightened yet? Check out our recruitment board.

  • Can I email anyone if I have a question?

    Yes you most certainly can -- and should! It doesn't matter if you just want to say hi, or you want to know how to derive the Clausius-Clayperon equation. We'll always reply.

Looking for something else? We'd like to help! Email us at hello@openlectures.org.
