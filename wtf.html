We believe that education should be free and accessible

And that it should look damn good while we're at it.

openlectures provides free online lectures based on the Singapore syllabus. It was founded in 2011 by Qiu Linan and Zhang Yitao, two students who strongly believed in making quality education free and accessible to every one.

Today, over a hundred staff and lecturers across the globe contribute their time and expertise to this effort. Everyone works for free, at nights, and on weekends, because we love what we do.
